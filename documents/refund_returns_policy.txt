ACME CORP — REFUND AND RETURNS POLICY
Effective Date: March 1, 2025 | Policy ID: POL-CS-007

1. OVERVIEW
Acme Corp is committed to customer satisfaction. This policy outlines the conditions under which customers may request refunds or product returns for software subscriptions and physical products.

2. SOFTWARE SUBSCRIPTIONS

Eligibility for Refund:
- Refund requests must be submitted within 14 days of the initial purchase.
- The product must not have been used for more than 5 active sessions.
- Annual plan purchases qualify for a pro-rated refund after the 14-day window, minus a 10% processing fee.
- Monthly plans are non-refundable after the 14-day grace period.

How to Request a Refund (Subscription):
1. Log in to your account at https://acmecorp.com/account
2. Navigate to Billing → Request Refund.
3. Select the subscription and reason for refund.
4. Submit the form; a confirmation email will be sent within 1 hour.
5. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.

3. PHYSICAL PRODUCTS

Return Window:
- Physical products (hardware, accessories) may be returned within 30 days of delivery.
- Items must be in original packaging and undamaged.
- Proof of purchase (invoice or order confirmation email) is required.

Non-Returnable Items:
- Opened software license keys.
- Products damaged by user misuse.
- Items marked "Final Sale" at the time of purchase.

Return Process:
1. Email returns@acmecorp.com with your order number and reason.
2. Acme Corp will send a prepaid return shipping label within 2 business days.
3. Pack the item securely and drop it at any courier location.
4. Once received, the refund is processed in 7–10 business days.

4. EXCHANGES
- Defective products qualify for an exchange within 60 days of purchase.
- Contact support@acmecorp.com with photos of the defect to initiate an exchange.

5. EXCEPTIONS AND SPECIAL CASES
- If service disruption is caused by Acme Corp infrastructure failure (downtime > 4 hours), affected customers receive a credit equal to 3x the downtime duration applied to their next billing cycle.
- Enterprise customers with a Service Level Agreement (SLA) should refer to their SLA document for specific remediation terms.
