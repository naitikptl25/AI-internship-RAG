STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE: Employee Onboarding
Document ID: SOP-HR-001 | Version: 2.1 | Last Updated: January 2025

1. PURPOSE
This SOP outlines the step-by-step process for onboarding new employees at Acme Corp to ensure a smooth, consistent, and compliant experience.

2. SCOPE
Applies to all full-time, part-time, and contract employees joining any department.

3. ONBOARDING STEPS

Step 1 – Pre-Joining (HR, 3 days before start date)
- Send offer letter and digitally signed employment contract.
- Share IT access request form to provision laptop, email, and Slack.
- Send welcome email with first-day schedule and office address.
- Assign a buddy from the same department.

Step 2 – Day 1 Activities
- Welcome meeting with HR (9:00 AM – 9:30 AM).
- IT setup: receive laptop, set up email account, install required software.
- Office tour and introduction to team members.
- Review and sign: NDA, code of conduct, data privacy acknowledgment.
- Complete mandatory compliance training (link sent via email).

Step 3 – Week 1 Training
- Shadow buddy for role-specific tasks.
- Attend department overview session with manager.
- Complete online security awareness training (mandatory, due by Day 5).
- Set up 1:1 meetings with direct manager every Friday.

Step 4 – 30-Day Check-in
- HR schedules a 30-minute feedback call.
- Manager completes initial performance baseline form.
- Employee reviews and updates personal goals document.

4. SYSTEM ACCESS
- Email & Calendar: Google Workspace (IT provisions within 24 hours).
- Project Management: Jira (IT provisions Day 1).
- Internal Wiki: Confluence (self-serve via SSO on Day 1).
- HR System: BambooHR (HR team provisions on Day 1).
